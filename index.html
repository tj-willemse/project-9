Who are we?

Situated in Krugersdorp Gauteng South Africa, Leather Products was founded all the way back in the 1990s, Since then we have pride ourselves on creating premium leather goods that embody timeless elegance and unparalleled craftsmanship.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans, using only the finest quality leathers sourced from the best tanneries around South Africa.

Our dedication to excellence is reflected in every stitch, ensuring that our products are not only beautiful but also durable and long-lasting. Whether it's a sophisticated handbag, a sleek wallet, or a classic belt, our leather goods are designed to elevate your style and stand the test of time.

To us it is more than just a piece of leather.
